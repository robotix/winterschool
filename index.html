About Us

The Winter School for AI and Robotics is an annual workshop conducted by the Technology Robotix Society, IIT Kharagpur. It is a beginner-friendly one-stop solution for anyone looking to step into the world of robotics. Students will get to explore their interest in any one of the three verticals offered: Computer Vision and Autonomous Robotics through real-life applications.
