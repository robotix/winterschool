About Us

The Winter School for AI and Robotics is an annual workshop conducted by the Technology Robotix Society, IIT Kharagpur and sponsored by IEEE Student Branch. It is a beginner-friendly one-stop solution for anyone looking to step into the world of robotics. Students will get to explore their interest in any one of the three verticals offered: Computer Vision, Autonomous Robotics and Machine Learning through real-life applications.

Starting during the second half of March, the workshop will be held on weekends and offer a wide range of problem statements, mentored classes and guest lectures from experts across different fields and walks of life. Stay tuned for a large number of opportunities that will be coming your way soon!
