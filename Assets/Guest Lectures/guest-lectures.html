Guest Lectures

The guest lecture series, conducted at the end of the session, is an integral part of the Winter School of AI and Robotics. It plays a pivotal role in showing the students how the industry works. The guests are professors, researchers, and industry experts who have worked in various fields such as Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, etc.
